Unique Device ID

When you sign in for the first time, our system generates a unique device ID based on your browser and stores it with your account.

Single-Device Access

After registration, only the original device can access your account. Even if someone knows your username and password, sign-ins from other devices will be blocked.

Why This Matters

This protects paid downloads from unauthorized sharing and ensures each purchase is tied securely to one person.

Tip: If you upgrade or replace your device, contact etsmith2028@gmail.com to reset your device ID and regain access.
